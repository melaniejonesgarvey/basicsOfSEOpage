What is Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps improve user and server interaction with sites by implementing best practices for site structure and content.

Why is SEO important?

The following are five reasons why SEO should be implemented for your website:

  1. Unlike other marketing tools, such as TV, radio, print and display online ads, SEO cuts through the clutter to effectively reach target audiences.
  2. SEO offers a healthy ROI (Return on Investment).
  3. Studies show that the majority of people conduct online research prior to the point of purchase. SEO will aid in making your website visible during this research period to consumers that are actively seeking your product or service.
  4. Smart phones and internet access have become a part of our daily routines and with that comes the use of SEO on the move. Further evidence of this is that according to Google mobile search queries currently outnumber desktop search queries!
  5. If you are not actively engaging in SEO strategies, you are being bumped out of place by competitors as you read this website.
