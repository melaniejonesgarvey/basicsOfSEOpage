Overall Picture

SEO is a multifaceted procedure and the strategies all work together. For example, On-Site SEO includes Page Content, Site Structure and HTML while Off-site SEO includes utilizing Social Media and Online Talk. However, at the end of the day each is dependent on the other for a truly successful SEO presence.

Keywords

Brainstorm on inquiries potential consumers would type into a search engine in order to find your webpage and the results are your keywords. Creating a comprehensive keyword list is a corner stone to an effective SEO campaign because they will be organically utilized at every opportunity such as:

  • Page Content
  • Meta Tags
  • Title Tags
  • Heading Tags
  • Alternate Text

Page Content

Websites that contain useful and relevant information presented in an organized fashion will produce better SEO results. It’s that simple!

Meta Tags

The meta tag is located in the header section and can be utilized to include a description of your website. Search engines will scan your website description to decide whether or not it is relevant to the inquiry and might be listed on the SERP.

Title Tags

Title tags are also referred to as page titles and are placed between the title tags on a website’s HTML page. Title tags are important because they are often included by search engines on the SERP. In an effort to increase the amount of times your site appears on the SERP use different title tags for each page within your site.

Heading Tags

Heading tags are a tool to create an outline of page content that organizes it for optimal flow.

Alternate Text

Search engines cannot see images or multimedia nor can all users. With that in mind it is a best practice to use alternate text to label these items on your website.

Linking

The link to each page within your website should be tested for validity. Broken links can result lower PageRanks.

Valid Code

HTML and CSS for each page of your website should be checked for validation. There are many reasons to utilize accurate coding and in regards to SEO it can help your ranking on the SERP.
